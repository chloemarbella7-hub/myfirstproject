What We Do

Services Built On Two Decades Of Craft

A complete, in-house building service for villa owners and investors across the Costa del Sol — from the first sketch to the final key handover.

01

New Build Villas

From a bare plot to a turnkey home: our in-house architects design contemporary and classic villas tailored to your lifestyle, then our construction team builds it to the highest specification, supervised personally from groundwork to final finish.

  • Bespoke architectural design & planning
  • Structural engineering & site management
  • Turnkey delivery, fully furnished on request
02

Full Reforms & Extensions

Complete renovations, extensions and layout reconfigurations for existing homes, townhouses and apartments — modernising older properties without compromising on their original character.

  • Whole-property strip-out & reform
  • Room extensions & second-storey additions
  • Kitchen & bathroom remodelling
03

Design & 3D Renders

See your project before a single brick is laid. Our design team produces detailed layouts, architectural plans and photo-realistic 3D renders so you can visualise — and refine — every space.

  • Concept layouts & floor plans
  • Photo-realistic exterior & interior renders
  • Material & finish selection support
04

Building Permits

Navigating Spanish bureaucracy is one of the biggest headaches for overseas owners — we manage the entire licence and permit process with local town halls on your behalf.

  • Licencia de obra & town hall liaison
  • Technical project documentation
  • Certificate of occupancy on completion
05

Interior Design

Tailored interiors, finishes and furnishings that elevate every room to a five-star standard — coordinated alongside construction so the final result feels seamless.

  • Bespoke joinery & finishes
  • Lighting & furniture curation
  • Full furnishing packages
06

Pools & Landscaping

Swimming pools, gardens and outdoor living spaces designed for the Mediterranean lifestyle — built to the same standard of craftsmanship as the home itself.

  • Infinity & traditional swimming pools
  • Landscaped gardens & irrigation
  • Outdoor kitchens, terraces & pergolas

Have A Project In Mind?

Tell us what you're planning — new build, reform or just a single room — and we'll come back with honest, expert advice.
