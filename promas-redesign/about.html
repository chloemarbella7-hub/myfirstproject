About ProMas

Two Decades Of Building Trust On The Costa del Sol

Our Story

Founded On Craftsmanship And Care

ProMas Building was founded over 20 years ago with a simple belief: that building a home for a client should feel as considered and personal as designing one for ourselves. What began as a small team of builders in Marbella has grown into a full-service construction company trusted by homeowners and investors across the Costa del Sol.

Today, our multilingual team manages every stage of a project in-house — architecture, permits, construction, interiors and landscaping — so clients have a single point of contact from first sketch to final handover. We have delivered more than 150 villas, reforms and extensions, many for repeat clients who return to us project after project.

Leadership

Meet Andy Prozhmi, Founder

Andy founded ProMas Building with a hands-on philosophy that still defines the company today — he oversees every project personally, from the first site visit to the final walkthrough. His attention to detail and direct involvement give clients confidence that their home is being built exactly as promised.

Under Andy's leadership, ProMas has built a reputation for transparent communication, near-daily project updates, and a level of craftsmanship that has made the company a trusted name among Marbella's villa owners and property investors.
